## The Challenge

<!-- 
  Why is integrating robots into last-mile delivery hard?

  - Multiple stakeholders affected: station managers, dispatchers, customers, drivers
  - Transition design: how do you run mixed human + robot operations?
  - Edge cases at scale: what happens when a robot can't deliver?
