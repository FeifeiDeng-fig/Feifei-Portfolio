Designing across
dimensions.

I believe great design comes from understanding the full spectrum of human experience — not just pixels on a screen.

I'm Feifei Deng, a Senior UX Designer at Amazon Delivery Experience. I specialize in designing for complex systems that bridge the physical and digital world — from IoT smart home devices to autonomous delivery robots.

Before tech, I studied interior design and landscape architecture, which gave me a deep appreciation for spatial thinking, materials, and how people move through environments. That foundation shapes everything I design today.

At Amazon, I'm on the robotics team exploring the next generation of human-robot interaction design. Previously, I designed the Wyze Air Purifier experience and built safety dashboards and operational tools for Amazon's delivery partners.

When I'm not designing, I'm illustrating — my character drawings are a big part of who I am. I love USM furniture, good coffee, and finding the poetry in everyday systems.
